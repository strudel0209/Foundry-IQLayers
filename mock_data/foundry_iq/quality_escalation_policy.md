# POL-QA-003: Quality Escalation and Batch Investigation Policy
**Effective Date:** 2025-06-15 | **Department:** Quality Assurance

## 1. Batch Investigation Triggers
- Return rate for a single product exceeds 15% of units sold in any 14-day period
- Three or more stores report same defect type for same product within 7 days
- Any customer health complaint

## 2. Investigation Steps
1. **Batch Identification**: Trace product to manufacturing batch via lot number
2. **Supplier Contact**: Notify supplier within 24 hours (contact: supplier-relations@lakeshoreretail.com)
3. **Sample Testing**: Pull 10 units from each affected store for lab testing
4. **Root Cause Analysis**: Complete within 5 business days
5. **Resolution**: Implement corrective action within 10 business days

## 3. Communication
- Internal: Slack channel #quality-alerts for real-time updates
- Store managers: Email notification within 4 hours of escalation
- Customers: Proactive outreach if safety concern identified

## 4. Documentation
All investigations must be documented in the Quality Management System (QMS).
Post-mortem report required within 15 business days of resolution.
